Sport & Events

Discover local dog-friendly events, meetups, and fun activities to keep you and your dog active and connected.

Coursing Ability Tests

Tests that measure a dog’s natural instinct to chase and pursue moving targets, even if they are not traditional coursing breeds.

Hunting Companion Training

Training programs that prepare dogs for hunting tasks such as flushing, retrieving, and working closely with hunters in the field.

Lure Coursing Sport

Dogs chase a mechanically operated lure across a field, simulating the pursuit of prey. This sport is especially popular with fast, sight-driven breeds.

Trick Dog Skills

Focuses on teaching fun and impressive tricks such as rolling over, spinning, or fetching items. Great for mental stimulation and entertainment.

Agility Course Competition

Dogs navigate obstacle courses including tunnels, jumps, and weave poles. Agility improves coordination, speed, and the bond between dog and handler.

Rally Obedience Challenges

A fun sport combining obedience and agility where dogs follow a course with signs that instruct different tasks, encouraging teamwork and communication.

Speed & Sprint Performance

Activities centered around dogs that love to run fast. These events measure acceleration, stamina, and enthusiasm in short-distance runs

Scent Work & Detection Training

Dogs learn to identify and locate specific scents such as objects, people, or substances. This stimulates their natural instincts and builds mental focus.

Obedience Mastery Training

Programs designed to teach dogs discipline, responsiveness, and good manners. This includes commands like sit, stay, heel, and recall in various environments.

Advanced Tracking Skills

Focuses on dogs trained to follow scent trails left by people or animals. Tracking dogs are often used in search-and-rescue, law enforcement, and sport competitions.

Field Trials Excellence

Competitive events that evaluate a dog’s ability to perform hunting-related skills such as retrieving, pointing, and teamwork with handlers in outdoor settings.

Farm Dog Certification

Dogs trained and tested for farm work, including herding livestock, guarding property, and assisting with daily agricultural tasks. These certifications recognize dogs that excel in real farm environments.
